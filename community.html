Local Support Groups

Find support groups in your area

Many communities have local support groups for people exploring gender identity. These groups provide a safe space for discussion, friendship, and mutual support.

How to Find Groups:

  • Contact local LGBTQ+ centers
  • Check with local healthcare providers
  • Search online community boards
  • Ask at local colleges or universities

What to Expect:

  • Safe, confidential environment
  • Peer support and understanding
  • Shared experiences and stories
  • Practical advice and resources
