Figures, facts & Terms: you are NOT alone

Figures

1.4 million adults in the US are transgender; ratio higher among youth

Facts

International Human Rights:

The equality and nondiscrimination guarantee provided by international human rights law applies to all people, regardless of sex, sexual orientation and gender identity or "other status."

---FROM UN HUMAN RIGHTS FACTSHEET

Legal Protection:

Supreme Court. Bostock v. Clayton County, No. 17-1618, 590 U.S. ___ (2020).: civil rights legislation protects gay and transgender workers from workplace discrimination.
